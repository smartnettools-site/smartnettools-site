About WCAG Contrast Requirements

What is Color Contrast?

Color contrast ratio measures the difference in perceived brightness between foreground and background colors. Higher ratios mean better readability.

WCAG Standards:

  • Level AA (Minimum):
    • 4.5:1 for normal text (<24px or <18px bold)
    • 3:1 for large text (≥24px or ≥18px bold)
  • Level AAA (Enhanced):
    • 7:1 for normal text
    • 4.5:1 for large text
