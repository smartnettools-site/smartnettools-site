How to Access Free Certificates

Most platforms offer two options:

  • Audit Mode (Coursera): Free access to course materials. You can learn everything but won't receive a certificate unless you pay.
  • Free Tier: Completely free access including certificates (AWS Skill Builder, Microsoft Learn, Cisco Skills for All, etc.)
  • Financial Aid: Many platforms offer financial aid for certificates if you can't afford them.

Note: All links provided go directly to official sources. We regularly verify these certifications remain free and accessible.
