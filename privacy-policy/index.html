Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

SmartNet Tools ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools at smartnettools.site (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form or request advice, you may provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Website URL
  • Message content

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain information automatically when you visit our Service:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source

Tool Usage Data

Important: All tools on SmartNet Tools process data locally in your browser. This means:

  • The data you enter into our tools never leaves your device
  • We do not store, collect, or have access to the content you process
  • All calculations and transformations happen client-side
  • Your data remains private and secure

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our Service and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our tools
  • Send occasional updates (only if you opt-in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. These may include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Service to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our Service
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our advertising partners (Google AdSense, Adsterra, Montag Ads) to serve relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. For more information, see our Cookies Policy.

Third-Party Services

Advertising Partners

We use third-party advertising services to display ads on our Service:

  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history
  • Adsterra: May collect information about your device and browsing behavior
  • Montag Ads: Uses tracking technologies to deliver relevant advertisements

These partners have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how our Service is used. These services may collect information about your visit and usage patterns.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for data in transit
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure server infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Analytics data: Retained according to our analytics provider's policy
  • Tool usage data: Not retained (processed locally only)

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent was given

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our Service, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
