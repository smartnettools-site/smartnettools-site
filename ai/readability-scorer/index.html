About Readability Scorer

This readability scorer calculates multiple readability metrics to help you understand how easy your text is to read. Perfect for writers, educators, and content creators.

Metrics Included:

  • Flesch Reading Ease Score (0-100)
  • Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level
  • Average words per sentence
  • Average syllables per word
  • Estimated reading time
  • Complexity assessment
